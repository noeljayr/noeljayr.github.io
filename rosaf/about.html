ABOUT ROSAF

Road Safety Alert Foundation (ROSAF) is a Local Non-Profit Making Organization that strives to contribute towards reduction of road crashes in Malawi through civic education, advocacy, social behavior change, and social mobilization among all classes of road users.

ROSAF is a team of multi-disciplinary individuals who bring together unique skills and expertise to shape and implement various strategies that point to the common goal of drastically reducing road crashes in Malawi which has reached alarming levels. Founded in 2016, the ROSAF is registered under the Companies Act, NO. 15 of 2013.
