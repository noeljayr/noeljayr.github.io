Our Partners

Government

The Road Safety Alert Foundation (ROSAF) supports efforts to reduce road crashes in Malawi alongside state agents. ROSAF acknowledges and appreciates the existing work done by state agents to combat road crashes. As a law-abiding institution registered with the government, ROSAF collaborates with the Directorate of Road Traffic and Safety Services (DRTSS) and the Malawi Police Service Traffic Department to jointly campaign against road crashes. Although the partnership is in its early stages, it shows promise. ROSAF also participates as a guest in the Africa Road Safety Day, observed on the third Sunday of November each year.
