About Kaso Security Services

Kaso Security Services is a Malawian company offering excellent security services, focusing on long-term relationships with clients. Their professionals are pro-active, dedicated, competent, motivated, and highly trained, offering guarding, CCTV, alarms, investigations, dog patrols, and electric fence installation.

Established by Malawian entrepreneurs, the company offers security services with over four years of experience. Its personnel continuously acquire professional knowledge and skills to meet clients' needs.
