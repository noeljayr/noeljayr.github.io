FAQs

Below are some commonly asked questions about my work. f you have any other inquiries or would like to discuss a potential project, feel free to get in touch.

What is your design process like?

Methodical design process involves understanding customer needs, conducting research, developing concepts, prototyping, and fine-tuning final designs, utilizing cooperative strategies for effective implementation.

Do you work with a team or as an individual developer?

I've had experience working both as an independent developer and as a member of a collaborative team. I adjust to the demands of the project and the client's preferences. Whether it's a group effort or a solo undertaking. For the most part I really enjoy being on the Frontend developer team when it's a team project, but I can do backend too. Versatility is the key!

What sets your approach apart from others?

I prioritize the demands and preferences of the end users because I adhere to the user-centric design philosophy. I put a lot of effort into making designs that are not just aesthetically pleasing but also simple to use and engaging for users.

What design software do you use?

I am proficient in a variety of design tools, including Figma, the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator) and Lucidchart In most cases, I start by editing images with Photoshop; if no images available, but the images are essential. I use Illustrator to create simple, flat illustrations I use Figma to combine everything into a beautiful UI. Lucidchart database schemas (database design), userflow diagrams and database design.
