Why Lalibela Boutique

Location

Lalibela boutique hotel is situated in the peaceful and Tranquil suburbs in Malawi's capital city of Lilongwe. Our hotel is just a stone's throw from city mall roundabout and offers guests a safe, quiet, and executive Environment.

Amenities

Our en-suite rooms are located on both the ground and upper floors of the hotel and comply with the latest standards for health, safety, and hygiene. Our outdoor pool, on-site restaurant provide guests with a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Special Promotions

We offer special promotions and offers during international and national holidays, from discounted room rates to complimentary meals and activities.
