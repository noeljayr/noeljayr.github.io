Physician Assistants Union Malawi (PAUM)

We serve as a union for individuals who work in the healthcare field as Clinical Technicians, Clinical Officers, Clinical Associates, and Medical Assistants. These individuals are registered members of the union and have access to the benefits and resources that it provides, such as representation, advocacy, and support for their professional development. The union is based in Malawi and serves the healthcare community in that country.
