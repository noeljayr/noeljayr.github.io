Commit to making a difference We are committed to making a difference in peoples lives. By making a donation to our organization, you can help us directly support those in need of improved access to health care and education.

How we use your donation We use the donations to provide resources and support for members to access continued education, We use the funds to support legal representation for members in disputes with their employers or to lobby for changes to laws and regulations that affect the rights of its members, use donations to provide resources and support for members to access continued education.

Donation is not only limited to funds Donation can be in form of resources, equipments, education materials, you can volunteer to assist with organizing events, providing mentorship, or contributing to outreach programs. By donating your skills and time to the union, you are helping to ensure that healthcare professionals in Malawi have the support they need to succeed.

Do your part Doing your part to support the union is an important way to help healthcare professionals in Malawi. Donating funds is a great way to contribute, but there are other ways to give too. By doing your part to help the union, you are showing your support for healthcare professionals in Malawi and helping to ensure that they have the support they need to succeed. Every contribution, no matter how big or small, makes a difference and helps the union reach its goals.

More Ways To Give There are many ways to contribute to the union and make a difference in the lives of healthcare professionals in Malawi. By donating your time, skills , you are helping to create a brighter future for healthcare professionals in the country.
