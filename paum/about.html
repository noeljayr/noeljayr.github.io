Physician Assistants Union of Malawi - Paum

Representing all Clinical officers/Associates

The Physician Assistants Union of Malawi (PAUM) is dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of its members as outlined in their contracts of employment, national laws, and international labor agreements to which Malawi is a party. Our mission is to ensure that our members have access to just and proper working conditions, and that the public and general health service in Malawi is vibrant and well-maintained.

We advocate and defend for all rights and interests of our members, including their right to continued education. We also affiliate with and subscribe to other trade unions or organizations that share our aims and objectives. PAUM acts as a focal point and mouthpiece for the interests of our members on matters of concern arising and as a negotiating body between our members and their respective employers.!

PAUM also represents its members on public and private bodies and committees or taskforces where such representation is necessary. We take part in all disciplinary issues involving our members and strive to resolve disputes between members and/or with their employers or the public. Furthermore, we provide various trainings for our members to ensure that they have the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their careers.
